Community Bulletin

Welcome to our monthly community bulletin! We have some exciting news for all residents. The local council has approved the new park renovation project, which will commence next month. This project aims to bring more green spaces and recreational facilities to our neighborhood. We encourage everyone to participate in the upcoming town hall meeting to share your thoughts.

In other news, our annual summer festival is just around the corner! Volunteers are needed for various activities including stall management, crowd control, and event setup. This is a great opportunity to give back to the community and meet new people. Please sign up using the contact form below if you are interested in volunteering. Let's make this year's festival the best one yet!
