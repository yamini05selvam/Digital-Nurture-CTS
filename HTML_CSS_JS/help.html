Community Portal Help Guide

Welcome to the Local Community Event Portal Help Guide.

  • Events: Check out past events in the image gallery. Double click to enlarge images.
  • Registration: Fill out the form in the Contact section to register for upcoming events. Don't forget your preferences are saved locally so you don't have to choose your event type again!
  • Location: Use the "Find Nearby Events" button on the home page to detect your coordinates and find local happenings.
